Bean

Bean's goal is to let people discover the different layers of taste that coffee offers.

The App

Bean looks at coffee the same way we look at wine tasting. Discovering different layers of flavour that can be found in different coffee varieties and roasts.

The app focuses on 4 main items:


  • different types of coffees
  • different types of brewing methods
  • interviews with people that have a passion for coffee
  • the 3 types of bean roasts

From the menu, the screen can take you to a sub-menu of each category. There you'll find a list of the items you can find out more about.

The last screens foucs on each item individually, letting you know in depth what flavours a certain coffee or brewing methods brings and each screen has an illustration.

Interviews

Mark & Bridie from Established

Mark and Bridie opened Established together back in December 2013, in Cathedral Quarter Belfast. The premise for opening the coffee shop was around feeling that there wasn’t any really great specialty coffee focused cafés in Belfast. Having a passion for coffee, customer service and food, and being stuck with the idea for a while, they decided it was time to open the coffee shop that they felt they needed themselves.

About the Bean App

The goal of this app is to show people that there’s more to coffee than just caffeine and maybe inspire them to explore a bit more and find a coffee type that they actually enjoy. We want for them to fall in love with coffee just like we did! So no matter where you are on your coffee journey, get exploring.
